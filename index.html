About

Naruto Uzumaki is the titular protagonist of the series. He was the first character created bu Kishimoto during the conception of the series and was designed with many traits from other shonen characters.

Naruto was serialized in Shueisha's magazine, Weekly Shōnen Jump from 1999 to 2014, and released in tankōbon (book) form in 72 volumes. The manga was adapted into an anime television series produced by Pierrot and Aniplex, which broadcast 220 episodes in Japan from 2002 to 2007; the English dub of the series aired on Cartoon Network and YTV from 2005 to 2009.

Team Kakashi (カカシ班 Kakashi-Han) or Team 7 (第7班, Dainanahan) is a fictional team in the manga and anime series Naruto. Told, this team is a team formed after Naruto, Sakura, and Sasuke passed the genin.

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki (うずまきナルト, Uzumaki Naruto) is a Ninja from Konohagakure. He became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of his birth — a fate that caused him to be shunned by most of Konoha throughout his childhood. After joining Team Kakashi, Naruto worked hard to gain the village's acknowledgment all the while chasing his dream to become Hokage.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha (うちはサスケ, Uchiha Sasuke) is one of the last surviving members of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. After his older brother, Itachi, slaughtered their clan, Sasuke made it his mission in life to avenge them by killing Itachi. He is added to Team 7 upon becoming a ninja and, through competition with his rival and best friend, Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke starts developing his skills.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura Haruno (うちはサクラ, Haruno Sakura, née Haruno (春野)) is a kunoichi of Konohagakure. When assigned to Team 7, Sakura quickly finds herself ill-prepared for the duties of a shinobi. However, after training under the Sannin Tsunade, she overcomes this, and becomes recognised as one of the greatest medical-nin in the world

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake (はたけカカシ Hatake Kakashi) is a fictional character in the manga and anime series Naruto. He is the 6th Hokage and also the teacher of Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura. He is also known as "Kakashi the imitation ninja", and it is said that the number of moves he has reached a thousand moves. The name Kakashi means scarecrow.

About Naruto

Naruto (Japanese: NARUTOナルト) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The story is told in two parts – the first set in Naruto's pre-teen years, and the second in his teens.
